Harvest

Why this bean behaves differently

Bharmour rajma is smaller than the plains variety and takes a long season, with cold nights building a fine skin that goes soft without bursting.

That is the whole trick. It holds its shape through a slow braise and still turns creamy inside.

How to cook it

  • Soak overnight in plenty of cold water.
  • Pressure cook for 20 to 25 minutes.
  • Salt only once the beans are nearly soft.

Provenance

Bharmour and the Chamba slopes

Smallholder dryland plots, steep enough that a tractor cannot go, produce beans with long-season depth and a clean, creamy finish.
