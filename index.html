Provenance

Where It Comes From

2,000 – 2,900 m Elevation

Chamba & Bharmour

Home to our Premium Rajma. These small, thin-skinned mountain beans are cultivated in the crisp air of the Bharmour region, resulting in a rich, buttery texture impossible to replicate at lower altitudes.

1,800 – 3,200 m Elevation

Kullu Valley

The cradle of the Guchhi. Fruiting briefly in March just after the snowmelt, these rare morels are wild-foraged by the same families who have walked these ancient forest paths for generations.

1,400 – 2,400 m Elevation

Shimla Hills

Nourished purely by rain and snowmelt, our Himalayan Red Rice is grown in the ancient terraced fields of the Shimla Hills, preserving an agricultural legacy that respects the mountain's natural rhythm.
